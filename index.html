About Us

Your Journey to Wellness Begins Here

EDE Wellness Studio (Every Day Eve Wellness Studio) is a Wellness studio offering Bikram Hot Yoga, deep tissue massage, power yoga, Lunch Time Aerobics, acupuncture and Massage Therapy with Cupping.

Explore Our Services

Who Are We

EDE Wellness Studio is a supportive community offering shared experiences and positive energy to nurture your wellness journey. Every visit fosters connection, encouragement, and deep, lasting transformation, ensuring you leave stronger, aligned, and renewed. Together, we grow, heal, and thrive.

Our Mission

To create a supportive space where individuals grow stronger—physically, mentally, and emotionally—through transformative practices like Bikram Yoga, Hot Pilates, aerobics, and therapy. We are here to guide every step of your wellness journey.

Our Objectives

EDE Wellness Studio promotes holistic wellness through diverse classes and therapies, fosters a supportive community, and delivers exceptional services—Bikram Yoga, Hot Pilates, aerobics, therapy—led by skilled professionals. We inspire consistent self-care and empower personal transformation, leaving members renewed and uplifted.

Meet Our Leadership

Evelyn Wasike

CEO & Director

Evelyn Wasike, founder of EDE Wellness Studio, transformed her life through Bikram Yoga in 2021. With a master’s in Diagnostic Radiography and leadership at Kenyatta National Hospital, she built EDE from scratch, overcoming challenges. Passionate about health, she champions wellness through yoga, fitness, and healthy living, inspiring others with resilience.

Nigel Henry

Manager & Head of Operations

Nigel Henry, Manager and Head of Operations at EDE Wellness Studio, drives strategic growth and daily management under its “Soulful Fitness, Soulful Living” mission. A Strathmore Law student, he applies legal expertise to strengthen operations. With precision, discipline, and leadership, he ensures smooth wellness program execution, fueling EDE’s success and transformation.
